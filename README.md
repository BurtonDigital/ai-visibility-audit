Your 3 Most Important Fixes
1. Add a FAQ page to your website
Why this matters: AI tools are built to answer questions. When someone asks "how much does a roof replacement cost in Bentonville," AI looks for businesses that have already answered that question on their website.
How to do it: Add a FAQ page with 8–10 questions: "How much does a roof replacement cost?", "How long does a roof job take?", "Do you work with insurance claims?", "Do you offer free estimates?", "What areas do you serve?"
Time to complete: 2–3 hours
Impact on AI visibility: High
2. Start responding to your Google reviews
Why this matters: AI tools notice whether business owners engage with their customers online. Responding to reviews signals that you're an active, trustworthy business. Right now, none of your 47 reviews have a response.
How to do it: Open Google Maps, search your business name, and click "Respond" on your most recent 5 reviews. Going forward, respond to every new review within a week.
Time to complete: 45 minutes to catch up, 5 minutes per review going forward
Impact on AI visibility: Medium
3. Get listed on BBB and Houzz
Why this matters: AI tools trust businesses that show up in multiple reputable directories. BBB and Houzz are two that AI tools commonly reference when recommending contractors.
How to do it: Go to bbb.org/businesses and create a free listing. Go to houzz.com/pro and create a free profile. Make sure your business name, address, and phone are identical across all platforms.
Time to complete: 1–2 hours
Impact on AI visibility: Medium
